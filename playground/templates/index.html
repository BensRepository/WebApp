What is CSTools?

This website is a personal project being developed for fun to combine my intrests in Video Games and Computer Science. I have created several tools for Counter-Strike which you can use at absolutely no cost and devoid of any intrusive advertisements.


CSTools began development 24th of April 2023 and continues to receive regular updates to increase functionality and implement new features. As a one-person initiative, I warmly welcome any insights or recommendations you might wish to share, for they are genuinely appretiated and actively sought.
